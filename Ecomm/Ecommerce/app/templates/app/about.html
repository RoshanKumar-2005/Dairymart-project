About SR Dairy

SR Dairy is committed to delivering fresh, high-quality dairy products produced under strict hygiene and quality controls.

We offer milk, curd, paneer, ghee, cheese and flavored milk — carefully processed and tested to meet nutritional standards.

How it started

SR Dairy began as a small family venture in 2025 with a simple mission: bring fresh, wholesome dairy from trusted local farms to nearby communities. Starting with a single pasteurisation unit and a handful of local suppliers, the founders focused on hygiene, transparency and fair pricing. Over the years the business grew — investing in modern processing, expanding the product range and partnering with retailers — while keeping its original commitment to quality.

  • Locally sourced milk from trusted farms
  • Hygienic processing & quality testing
  • Sustainable packaging and timely delivery
